New York

The Brooklyn Bridge

There are several bridges in Manhattan but none more popular than the Brooklyn Bridge. It it is an inspiration to many artists and symbolism of man's industry for many.

Daily, thousands walk through this bridge to witness it's magnificence.

The Iconic Manhattan Skyline

This beautiful skyline is one of the most recognized in the world. On all sides of Manhattan you have an endless array of Architectural marvels including The Freedom Tower, The Emire State Building, and The Chrysler Building.

It is truly awe-inspiring.

Times Square

This is one of the cultural centers of the world. From this central Manhattan location comes legendary broadway shows like The Lion King, Cats, Phantom of the Opera and more.

This is also the location of the biggest party in the world. Hundreds of thousands of people gather in this relatively tiny spot to greet the new year and see the annual ball drop.

This is the symbol of the city that never sleeps.

Statue of Liberty

A gift from France, this magnificent statue stand tall south of Manhattan to greet people from all over the world.

Lady Liberty is the symbol of what this country is about - a place where anybody can make a life of their own...to live the American Dream.

San Francisco

San Francisco Exploratorium

Beautiful facility with amazing views. Ideal way to spend the day with your kids andentire family. Brings the kid out in everyone of all ages.

Golden Gate Bridge

Iconic location well worth the visit. Can't overstate beauty of this historic bridge. The park is great and there's so much to see nearby.

Fisherman's Wharf

This street makes your trip to San Francisco worth it. There are entertainers, restaurants, samples, musicians, attractions! Everything to see and do is located here!!

Alcaltraz Island

This Place is amazing! Fantastic views of the city and scape tour was fantastic with a very animated Ranger that had lots of extra nuggets of facts and stories.

Tucson

Downtown Tucson

Downtown is in the heart of Tucson, where everyone goes for food, drinks and excitement!

Saguaro National Park

Arizona's Saguaro National Park protects and shelters Tucson's wildlife, draws hardy hikers and horseback riders. (National Geographic).

Fox Theater

Fox Theather is a place that offers high quality performing arts, cultural activities and films in order to inspire, excite, entertain, engage and educate guests.(Fox Theather)

4th Avenue

Like downtown Tucson, 4th Avenue is the best place to go to dine, drink, dance, and experience Tucson's culture.
